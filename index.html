Welcome to Claybymarienne

We craft timeless clay pieces that celebrate African heritage, culture, and everyday beauty.

Our Mission

We produce claywares to help improve your health and wellbeing through their natural benefits—detox, anti-cancer, anti-hypertensive, antidiabetic, biodegradable, and antiviral properties. Our mission is also to reduce the impact of global warming by eliminating plastic and other synthetic materials from daily use.

Our Story

ClayByMarrienne was born from muddy hands, curious hearts, and a deep love for African expression. What started as a passion project has grown into a brand that honors tradition, embraces imperfection, and elevates everyday objects into keepsakes. Each piece is shaped with care and carries stories—of resilience, craft, and community.
